# ALMA Rev vertical campaign matrix

Use this when adapting the Revenue Engine Health Check or growth-bottleneck offer by vertical.

## Consulting
- Main pain: founder dependency, pipeline visibility, follow-up discipline
- Offer angle: find the revenue bottleneck hiding in plain sight
- Best CTA: low-friction ask to send the diagnostic
- Example framing: growth motion still depends too much on the owner

## CPA / advisory
- Main pain: capacity trapped in delivery, weak expansion motion, delayed follow-up
- Offer angle: where revenue leaks across delivery and expansion
- Best CTA: send a short health check
- Example framing: strong client work, but growth gets trapped inside delivery

## Legal
- Main pain: intake speed, case conversion, dormant lead reactivation
- Offer angle: where intake and follow-up are slowing signed matters
- Best CTA: ask if they want a tailored version
- Example framing: interest exists, but not enough signed matters

## Insurance
- Main pain: response speed, quote follow-up, renewals, dormant book reactivation
- Offer angle: where response and follow-up are weakening the revenue engine
- Best CTA: quick read on the company
- Example framing: activity exists, but slow response suppresses conversion

## Delivery rules
- Keep the asset in English for ALMA Rev outbound
- Use one CTA only per email
- Keep the offer line first, short and concrete
- For bundle requests like "Matriz inteira", deliver all verticals in one response, with separate headings per vertical and no extra strategy memo
- If a vertical is clearly different in pain or buyer psychology, split it into its own wave rather than blending it into a generic campaign
