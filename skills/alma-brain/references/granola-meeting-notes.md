# Granola meeting notes

## Purpose
Use Granola as a recurring source of meeting context, recaps, decisions, follow-ups, and names mentioned in calls Mario participates in.

## Where to look
- Brain vault path: `05-meeting-notes/granola/`
- Treat Granola notes as append-only meeting evidence, not as curated strategy.
- When a meeting recap conflicts with a canonical decision, prefer the newer canonical source (`STATUS.md`, `_CURRENT.md`, `decisions.md`) and treat the recap as supporting context.

## How to use it
1. Search for the person, account, project, or meeting theme.
2. Read the newest relevant recap first.
3. Extract only durable facts, open loops, and explicit decisions.
4. Feed those facts into daily status, follow-up, or memory updates when they are stable.

## Pitfalls
- Do not promote a recap to canonical truth by default.
- Do not overfit to a single meeting when multiple recaps point to a broader pattern.
- Do not use meeting notes as a substitute for live system state.

## Good triggers
- "what did we discuss with X"
- "where did we leave Y"
- "what meetings am I in"
- "summarize recent calls"
- "extract follow-ups from meeting notes"
