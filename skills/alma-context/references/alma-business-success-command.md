# ALMA business success command

Use when Mario gives a broad mandate like `make ALMA successful`, `arruma o negócio`, `faz isso rodar`, or asks for global business acceleration.

## Operating interpretation

Treat the request as an execution mandate, not as a strategy essay.

Default sequence:
1. Load brain status first: `STATUS.md`, then the relevant `_CURRENT.md` for ALMA Rev / Agentic.
2. Reconcile brain with live state before claiming anything: services, cron jobs, Instantly/campaign state, revenue-critical configs, and recently written operational files.
3. Identify the revenue gate, not the most interesting engineering work. Current default gates are:
   - outbound deliverability and mailbox readiness,
   - live campaign dispatch and replies,
   - AURA diagnosis path,
   - meeting booked / meeting done / diagnostic sold / retainer closed,
   - portal/site only when it blocks conversion or trust.
4. Split output into `estado`, `bloqueio`, `movimento agora`, `resultado esperado`, and `verify`.
5. Execute the next safe operational move immediately if credentials/tooling are available.
6. If a plan is useful, write it to brain under `agents/hermes/` as an operating artifact, not as a generic memo.

## What not to do

- Do not answer broad success mandates with motivational planning.
- Do not expand into every ALMA workstream equally. Rank by path to revenue.
- Do not treat old brain/project summaries as live truth. Use them as index, then verify.
- Do not spend time on labs or historical projects unless they directly affect the revenue gate.

## Final response pattern

Use a short verdict-first report:

- `veredito`: what is currently blocking success
- `feito agora`: concrete actions executed
- `próximo`: one or two moves that unlock revenue fastest
- `risco`: the main thing that can make the plan false

Keep it operational and evidence-backed.
